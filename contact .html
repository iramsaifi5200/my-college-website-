VMLG College Details

vidyawati mukund lal girls college,ghaziabad was established in 1961. The college is affiliated with chaudary charan singh university, meerut and is recognized by UGC. It offers various undergraduate and postgraduate courses including B.A,B.com,BCA,B.Ed.,M.A,M.com,and M.Ed. The college provides modern facilites such as library,computer labs,Smart classrooms,sports facilites ,cafeteria,wi-fi campus, and research centers for students.The institution is known for promoting quality education and women empowerment
